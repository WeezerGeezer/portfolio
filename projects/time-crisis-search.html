    Time Crisis Search

    AI-Powered Podcast Search Engine

    Built with Python, Supabase, OpenAI Whisper, and PostgreSQL

    View Live Site View Code

    The Problem

    As a longtime fan of Ezra Koenig's "Time Crisis" radio show, I found myself constantly trying to remember specific moments, jokes, or discussions from hundreds of hours of podcast content. The show has been running since 2015, covering everything from music industry insights to random cultural commentary, but there was no way to search through this treasure trove of content.

    The challenge wasn't just building a search engine‚Äîit was creating an entire pipeline to convert audio into searchable, accurate text while maintaining the context and conversational flow that makes the show special. I needed to solve speaker identification (distinguishing between Ezra, his co-host Jake, and various guests), handle varying audio quality, and create a search experience that felt natural and responsive.
